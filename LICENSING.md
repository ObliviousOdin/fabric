copyright and permission notice is preserved in
[LICENSES/MIT-nous-research.txt](LICENSES/MIT-nous-research.txt), as its terms

## Third-party components

Third-party components retain their own copyright and license notices in the
files shipped with those components. Component-level terms remain controlling
for their respective components. See
[THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md](THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md) for the full guidance.

## Redistributing Fabric

When redistributing Fabric, include the root `LICENSE`, the root `NOTICE`,
`THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md`, the `LICENSES/` directory, and all component-level
license and notice files.
